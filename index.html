The Invitation

Enter a new cycle
with intention

This weekend is an invitation to step away from the pace of daily life and into something more intentional. Together, we will move through ceremony, medicine work, bodywork, reflection, and connection, supported by the land and one another.

Each person who joins contributes to the depth of the experience, creating something that is both grounding and transformative. We are holding this experience for those who feel genuinely called to step into this kind of work at this time.
