The Invitation

You've been in motion.

Creating, producing, holding, pushing.
Not everything you've been carrying is meant to come with you into the next cycle.

El Salto Sagrado is a weekend of intentional transition—a space to pause, reflect, release, and step forward with clarity.

We gather at the intersection of two forces:

Rabbit Energy

Urgency, creation, expansion

Mictlantecuhtli

Endings, transformation, unseen guidance

This work is not about doing more. It is about releasing, realigning, and choosing consciously.

The Container

Who This Is For

This space is held for those ready to step fully into the work.

This is for you if:

  • You feel something in your life is no longer sustainable
  • You've been in constant motion and feel the cost of it
  • You are ready to release patterns, roles, or identities that no longer align
  • You want to take action—but in a way that is grounded, ethical, and intentional
  • You value ceremony, cultural reverence, and depth over surface-level wellness
  • You are open to being seen, honest, and accountable in community

This is not for you if:

  • You are looking for a relaxing spa-style getaway
  • You want constant entertainment or a packed schedule
  • You are not open to self-reflection or personal responsibility
  • You prefer purely physical wellness without emotional or spiritual depth
  • You are not ready to release anything yet
